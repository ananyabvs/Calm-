Why this works (science) Mindfulness helps shift attention to the present, reducing rumination and stress responses (Kabat-Zinn, 1990). Self-affirmation can protect a sense of self-worth during threat, improving coping and openness (Steele, 1988; Creswell et al., 2013). Expressive writing supports emotional processing and meaning-making, which can reduce distress over time (Pennebaker, 1997). Slow, structured breathing can influence autonomic balance and downshift physiological arousal (Zaccaro et al., 2018).
